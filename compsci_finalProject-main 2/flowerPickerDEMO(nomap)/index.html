Welcome to the home page for our final project!

This project is a flower recommendation website that suggests flowers based on YOUR feelings and the color you choose. Personalize a bouquet for a special someone by picking out a flower. Then visit our map to find a local flower shop to request a bouquet with said flower!



What feeling are you trying to express with this bouquet?



EX: Color: Green , Feeling: Love --> Green Carnation

EX: Color: empty , Feeling: Love --> Red Rose

EX: Color: Orange, Feeling: Love -(no exact match)-> Red Rose
